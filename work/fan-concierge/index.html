Sports Concierge


Central Question
How might we connect sports team to their fans even when events are not happening?


Skills

Digital Strategy, iOS Design, Framer Studio

At first we thought the end game was to get as many butts in their seats as possible. Monetizing a fan was about whether or not they were in the stadium. So naturally, the sports industry put a lot of focus on how to get their fans into their arenas. But what if you could connect with the fan during the week, when there isn't a game? What if the fan could easily connect with their favorite recap video, player, brand, or even purchase and exchange tickets on demand without searching for it?

We wanted to focus on three context -- pre-game, in-game, post-game. At the same time, we also needed to balance front-office needs and sponsorship opportunities.
